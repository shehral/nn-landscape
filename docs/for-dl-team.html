  • Three distinct behavioral VLM hallucination detection paradigms appear in one build: spatial evidence anchoring (NVIDIA VSS Blueprint), late-interaction retrieval over large corpora (ArtSeek), and RAG-based context-aware generation (Knowledge-Infused-Multimodal-Retrieval). All three operate without model internals access, distinct from the team's mechanistic interpretability work.
  • Both primary VLM benchmark harnesses used by IDP Leaderboard competitors (lmms-eval and evalscope) trend simultaneously while OCR-3 is absent from both model registries. Competitors can publish DocVQA or OmniDocBench comparison tables via either framework that structurally omit OCR-3 as a reference point.
