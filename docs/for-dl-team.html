Patterns this build

  • The complete open-source document AI substitution stack appears in a single cycle: MinerU and opendataloader-pdf for extraction, Unstructured for ETL, RAGFlow and Dify for orchestration, lmms-eval and evalscope for evaluation, ms-swift for fine-tuning. No single component is new; the pattern is simultaneous trending, suggesting developer adoption of the whole stack is accelerating.
  • Evaluation frameworks (lmms-eval, evalscope) trend alongside fine-tuning toolchains (ms-swift, LLaVA-OneVision-1.5) and local inference (Ollama with GLM-5.1 and Qwen), completing the external-lab pipeline for IDP Leaderboard submissions. The threat is an industrialized submission pipeline, not a single new model.
  • MCP is consolidating as the connector protocol across two distinct deployment tiers: UltraRAG (community MCP-native RAG) and genkit (Google-backed enterprise SDK). Both appear this cycle without Nanonets connectors, suggesting the protocol gap is widening across developer segments simultaneously.
  • Two complementary VLM hallucination mitigation paradigms appear in the same cycle: explanation-faithfulness auditing (crossroute-audit, post-hoc) and retrieval-based hallucination mitigation (Knowledge-Infused-Multimodal-Retrieval, generation-time). Both are distinct from mechanistic interpretability, suggesting the research community is exploring multiple mitigation layers in parallel rather than converging on a single approach.
