Patterns this build

  • Three independent open-source projects (MinerU, opendataloader-pdf, RAGFlow) all target the PDF-to-structured-output pipeline that commercial extraction APIs occupy; the category is commoditizing via GitHub-distributed tooling.
  • Two distinct multimodal evaluation toolkits (lmms-eval, evalscope) trending simultaneously with overlapping DocVQA, ChartQA, and IDP-adjacent benchmark coverage; the community is standardizing around these tasks as the shared evaluation substrate.
  • ms-swift adds GRPO training for Qwen3-VL and GLM-5.1 while LLaVA-OneVision-1.5 lowers multimodal fine-tuning friction; the combined effect could produce new document-VLM IDP Leaderboard entries within weeks.
  • Two independent projects (self-reflective-hallucination-aware-multimodal-rag, Knowledge-Infused-Multimodal-Retrieval) address multimodal hallucination via retrieval augmentation rather than model internals; a retrieval-side mitigation paradigm is emerging in parallel to mechanistic interpretability approaches.
