Patterns this build

  • Three distinct open-source PDF parsers targeting the LLM-ready data interface (MinerU, opendataloader-pdf, OCRmyPDF) are trending in the same cycle, signaling accelerating commoditization of the parsing tier below the structured-extraction product layer.
  • Retrieval-based VLM hallucination mitigation is establishing a parallel paradigm alongside mechanistic interpretability: three items (self-reflective-rag, Knowledge-Infused-Multimodal-Retrieval, mimo-docagent) address phantom-row and structural hallucination classes via retrieval augmentation or long-context reasoning rather than causal methods.
  • Two multimodal evaluation frameworks (lmms-eval, evalscope) are trending with IDP-adjacent benchmark coverage and registered competitive models, but without OCR-3; each additional cycle of non-registration increases the risk that competitors publish comparisons that structurally exclude OCR-3.
  • Four distinct agentic workflow orchestration platforms (Dify, RAGFlow, Flowise, UltraRAG) are trending simultaneously, each bundling its own document parser defaults; the orchestration layer is growing more crowded without consolidation, expanding the surface where a Nanonets connector gap compounds.
  • Omni-modality serving infrastructure is forking from standard LLM serving (vllm-omni splits from vLLM for vision-audio-text batching) while general-purpose multimodal serving (SGLang) continues advancing; the two tracks signal that omni-modality models have distinct serving requirements emerging at the infrastructure level.
