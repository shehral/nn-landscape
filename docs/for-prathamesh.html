  • Tesseract (legacy OCR baseline) trends alongside advanced VLM training tooling (ms-swift, LLaVA-OneVision-1.5) in the same cycle, indicating the developer population spans a wide capability range simultaneously — tutorial activity at the accuracy floor and GRPO-tuned production VLMs at the ceiling.
